Welcome to MyCalcTools

MyCalcTools is a free collection of 46 practical online tools made for everyday questions, quick planning and simple comparisons. The site includes calculators for health and body, finance and money, kitchen tasks, lifestyle and dates, business and creator work, eco planning and unit conversion.

Easy to follow tools

Each tool includes clear inputs, simple results and easy-to-follow user instructions so you can understand what to enter, what the result means and when the calculator is useful. Most tools are designed for quick browser-based estimates with no sign-up required.
