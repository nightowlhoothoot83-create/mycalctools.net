Use About MyCalcTools when you need a quick answer without creating an account, downloading an app or sending your information to a third party. The tool is designed for everyday planning, comparison and checking, with results shown in the browser as soon as you enter the details.

How to use About MyCalcTools

  1. Enter the dates, numbers or options requested by the form on this page.
  2. Choose any unit, country, time period or setting that applies to your situation.
  3. Press the calculate or generate button to show the result.
  4. Review the explanation beside the result before relying on it for planning.

When this page is useful

About MyCalcTools is useful for quick checks, household planning, school or work tasks, content planning and comparing simple scenarios. Many visitors use these tools to avoid manual counting or spreadsheet setup for a one-off question.

The result should be treated as a practical guide rather than professional advice. For legal, financial, health, tax, pregnancy, school holiday, tide or public holiday decisions, confirm important details with an official source or qualified professional.

Privacy and accuracy

MyCalcTools tools run in your browser wherever possible. We aim to keep pages clear, lightweight and easy to read on mobile, tablet and desktop. If a page uses dates, public holidays or calendar rules, those rules can change over time, so checking the final result against the relevant authority is recommended for high-stakes decisions.
