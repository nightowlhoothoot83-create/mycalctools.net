About MyCalcTools

MyCalcTools is a free online calculator suite built by Ascension Digital — a multi-brand digital content and commerce group based in Australia.

Built for everyday Australians who just need the number, without the noise.

What We Built

38 free calculators across health, finance, kitchen, lifestyle, business and eco planning. Every tool is built to be fast, accurate, and honest — with no paywalls, no email collection, and no dark patterns.

The Australian finance tools are aligned to ATO FY2024-25 tax brackets. The eco tools use BOM rainfall and state-based solar data. All tools run entirely in your browser — nothing is sent to a server.
